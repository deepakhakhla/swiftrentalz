Frequently Asked Questions

General Queries

Q: What is SwiftRentalz?

A: SwiftRentalz is a trusted platform connecting car owners with customers seeking rental services. We ensure smooth transactions and verified service providers.

Q: Is registration mandatory?

A: Yes, both service providers and renters must register to ensure transparency and security.

Payment & Booking

Q: What payment options are available?

A: We support credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular wallets for seamless transactions.

Q: How do I cancel a booking?

A: Go to your account dashboard, select the booking, and click on the cancel option. Cancellation policies may apply.
